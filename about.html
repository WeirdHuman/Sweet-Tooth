Our Story

Sweet-Tooth Tea House was first opened on 1996 in the heart of Northridge city. Sweet-Tooth was funded by beautiful people with LOVE and CARE.... At Sweet-Tooth Tea House, we strive to bring the signatures of the Taiwanese topioca bubble tea to the world. We sourced our ingridients with responsibility and love. Our products are one of the highest quality in the market. Furthermore, our bobas are made on-site so that they will always be fresh for our beloved customer. First, we always said that to start a company one day.... and we just did not think it was a boba bussiness!! And that's where are we today.. We wanted to change the way people view toward boba and tea..... We are greatful that you can join us in our journey...
